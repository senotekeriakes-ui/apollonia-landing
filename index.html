01 · The Camera
4K clinical video, zero disruption
Clinical documentation in dentistry hasn't kept pace with the rest of medicine. Procedures go unrecorded, tools remain disconnected, and magnification hardware hasn't changed in decades.

Record Every Procedure

The Apollonia Camera captures 4K footage of your dental treatments from a first-person perspective, building a complete visual record without changing how you work.

Fits Your Workflow

Designed to integrate seamlessly into your existing routine. No extra steps, no awkward positioning. Just mount it and treat. The camera disappears into your workflow.

Purpose-Built for Dentistry

Not a repurposed action camera or phone mount. Every detail, from the optics to the form factor to the controls, is engineered specifically for the dental operatory.

Review, Learn, Share

Use your recordings for case documentation, patient communication, peer review, or training. 4K clarity means you capture the clinical detail that matters.

Who We Are

Apollonia Medical Technology ("Apollonia", "we", "us", "our") is an Australian company developing clinical camera and software products for dental professionals. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with this website and apply for our beta programme.

Information We Collect

When you submit a beta application, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (if provided)
  • City of practice, professional role, and specialty (if applicable)
  • Information about your current loupes (if provided)
  • Free-text responses you choose to provide

We also automatically collect basic analytics data such as page views, referral source, and device type through standard web technologies. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and evaluate your beta application

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use trusted third-party service providers for form handling and email communications, each bound by their own privacy obligations. Data is stored in Australia or in jurisdictions with comparable data protection standards.

Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
